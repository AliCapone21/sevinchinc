Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

SEVINCH INC is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. What We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number — when you contact us through our form
  • Optional email from newsletter or hiring forms
  • Technical data like browser type and IP address (via cookies or analytics)

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to your inquiry or request
  • To improve our website experience through analytics
  • To occasionally notify you of services, only if you opted in

3. RingCentral Integration

Our service uses RingCentral's APIs to facilitate communication. When you connect your RingCentral account, we may access and use data such as:

  • -Your RingCentral account information
  • -Call and message metadata (not message content)
  • -Contact and directory information

We access only the data necessary for service functionality. This data is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party services (like Google Maps or analytics) that collect limited, anonymized data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us using the information below.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures (such as SSL and limited access) to protect your information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.
