Our Journey So Far

SEVINCH INC was founded in 2023 in Dayton, Ohio, by a team passionate about safe and reliable trucking services. What started as a single-truck operation quickly grew into a dependable regional carrier trusted by shippers across multiple states.

With dedication, clear communication, and consistent delivery performance, we’ve built a reputation based on integrity. Our mission is simple: deliver freight on time, every time — while supporting our drivers and clients with honesty and care.

Our Milestones

2022 – The Beginning

Founded in Dayton, OH with a single truck and a big vision. Focused on local deliveries and urgent load requests.

Currently – Expanding Operations

Expanded to serve more states. Built long-term partnerships, added dispatchers, and onboarded new drivers.

Looking Forward

With new technology, better tracking, and growing capacity, we aim to become a nationally trusted carrier brand.

About SEVINCH INC

SEVINCH INC is a full-service logistics company offering reliable freight services across the USA. We connect businesses with efficient and timely transportation solutions.
