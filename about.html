Who We Are

Founded in 2025, SEVINCH INC is a proud newcomer in the American logistics industry, based in Lewis Center, Ohio. Though young, we bring fresh energy, high standards, and a relentless commitment to reliability.

We specialize in full truckload (FTL), less-than-truckload (LTL), and expedited freight services across the United States. Backed by experienced dispatchers, dedicated drivers, and a modern fleet, our mission is to deliver goods safely, on time, and with care.

We understand what small businesses and shippers need — responsive communication, honest pricing, and dependable service. That’s what we aim to deliver, every mile of the way.
