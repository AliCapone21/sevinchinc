Who We Are

Founded in 2025, SEVINCH INC is a proud newcomer in the American logistics industry, based in Lewis Center, Ohio. Though young, we bring fresh energy, high standards, and a relentless commitment to reliability.

We specialize in full truckload (FTL), less-than-truckload (LTL), and expedited freight services across the United States. Backed by experienced dispatchers, dedicated drivers, and a modern fleet, our mission is to deliver goods safely, on time, and with care.

We understand what small businesses and shippers need — responsive communication, honest pricing, and dependable service. That’s what we aim to deliver, every mile of the way.

Our Key Logistics Services

Offering a complete range of freight and trucking solutions designed for your business.

Full Truckload (FTL) Shipping

We offer Full Truckload (FTL) shipping for large-volume freight that fills an entire trailer. This service ensures faster transit times, less handling, and direct delivery — ideal for time-sensitive or high-volume shipments.

Less Than Truckload (LTL) Shipping

Our LTL shipping service is perfect for smaller freight that doesn't require a full trailer. By sharing space with other shipments, you reduce costs while still receiving dependable delivery and tracking.

Dedicated Trucking Services

With our dedicated trucking services, a truck and driver are assigned exclusively to your business. This ensures consistent scheduling, increased reliability, and brand-specific logistics support tailored to your unique needs.

Route Optimization

Our advanced route planning solutions minimize delays, reduce fuel costs, and ensure your deliveries stay on time and on budget.

Fleet Management

Optimize your transportation operations with our fleet management solutions, offering real-time tracking, maintenance scheduling, and performance insights.

Real-Time Shipment Tracking

Track your shipments in real-time with full visibility from origin to destination. Stay informed with automated updates and alerts.
